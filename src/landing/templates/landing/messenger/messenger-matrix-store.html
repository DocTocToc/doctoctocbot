Bridges

Bridges mean that you can remain in your Element while talking to people stuck in proprietary walled gardens, or on a different network.

Slack. Connect Slack channels and Matrix rooms for native interoperability.

MS Teams. For native interoperability between MS Teams and Matrix.

Gitter. For native interoperability between Gitter and Matrix.

IRC. Dynamically bridge IRC channels and synchronise user-lists.

Discord. Connect text-based Discord channels and Matrix rooms.

Telegram. For interoperability between Telegram and Matrix.

Contact us to find out about additional bridges which may be available soon, or as an add-on for your Element Matrix Services (EMS) server.

Bots

Bots interact with third-party applications directly from Element. They sit in your rooms to listen and respond to your command. Use them to boost team productivity or add to the fun.

Google image search. Search for any image online, from within Element.

Giphy. Invoke Giphy to produce the most appropriate GIF in your chat.

Guggy. Turn your text into personalised GIFs.

GitHub. Puppet your GitHub account straight from your chat room.

RSS Bot. Get your RSS feeds straight into your chat room.

Imgur Image Search. Search Imgur for relevant images without leaving Element.

Travis CI. Receive all your build results straight into the discussion, to boost team productivity.

Wikipedia. Search Wikipedia without leaving Element.

Contact us to find out about additional bots which may be available, or request an add-on.

Widgets

Widgets embed full-blown interactive apps into your rooms. Everyone in a room can access them - helping you share or collaborate around the same material.

Etherpad. For shared note taking.

Grafana. The must-have for any devops; your dashboard straight in Element.

Google calendar. Share your calendar within Element.

Jitsi. Free unlimited voice and video conferences, alongside your chat.

Spotify. Control your music straight from Element.

Stickerpack. Make your chat more fun with stickers.

YouTube. Watch videos together, without leaving your chat.

Ethercalc. Embed editable spreadsheets.

TradingView. Keep an eye on your exchange rates while working from Element.

Contact us to find out about additional widgets which may be available soon, or request an add-on.
